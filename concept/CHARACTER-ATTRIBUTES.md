# Character Attributes - the classification schema (GameOS module)

A quick, structured **tag sheet** every character gets. It complements CHARACTER-METHOD (which is
the deep narrative) - this is the *skeleton of classification* that keeps the roster **unique**
(aim for a distinct combo per character), **consistent** (every axis defined the same way),
**varied** (the roster matrix shows gaps - "everyone's physical, add a mage"), and **expandable**.

Fill a character's tags right after their nature is locked; revisit the roster matrix before
adding a new fighter to deliberately fill a gap.

## The axes (pick one value per axis; add values as needed)
1. **Nature / kind** - human · beast-kin · creature · alien · construct/golem · spirit/yokai ·
   elemental · undead · divine/demon · **transformed** (was one thing, became another).
2. **Power source** - physical/martial · elemental · magic (mage) · tech/gadget · psychic/mind ·
   divine/cursed · innate mutation · none (pure skill).
3. **Combat archetype** (mechanics) - rusher · control/zoner · tank/bruiser · grappler ·
   glass-cannon · trapper/setup · all-rounder.
4. **Range** - close/melee · mid · long/ranged · mixed.
5. **Movement mode** - grounded · floating/hover · flying · teleport/blink · dash-heavy.
6. **Tempo / playstyle** - aggressive · defensive · patient-control · hit-and-run · setup.
7. **Temperament** (personality register) - e.g. hot/loud/warm · cold/quiet/composed · wild ·
   arrogant · stoic · playful.
8. **Size / silhouette class** - small-nimble · average · tall-lean · large-heavy.
9. **Story role** - rookie · veteran · rival · gatekeeper · champion/boss · wildcard · comic.
10. **Origin / era** - present · past (displaced) · future · otherworld · unknown.
11. **Element / theme** - fire · ice · lightning · earth · wind · void · light · nature · none.
12. **Moral vibe** - heroic · anti-hero · cold-neutral · villain · chaotic.

## Rule of thumb
No two characters should share too many axes - if a new fighter matches an existing one on
Nature+Power+Archetype, rethink. Diversity across axes = a roster that feels big.
