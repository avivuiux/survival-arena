# Art-Depth Pass - the module (extract rich, detailed, professional art)

A GameOS module. After a character's identity is locked (CHARACTER-METHOD), run this to mine for
**complex, detailed, professional, creative, stunning** visual detail before generating art.
Asked as our native **structured questions** (one option-set per bucket), each answer feeds the
generation prompt directly. Validated live on ZERO (2026-07-01) - it's what turned "ice guy" into
an intricate cosmic-alien ice being.

## The buckets (ask each as a structured question, curated options per character)
- **A. Shape language / silhouette** - which shapes define them; what the silhouette says in 1s;
  the one silhouette-hook only they have.
- **B. Material & texture** - what each part is MADE of and in what condition; the rough-vs-smooth
  contrast. (Most of the "detail" lives here.)
- **C. History written on the body** - every scar/crack/stain/repair: what happened; what the body
  and gear "remember."
- **D. The hero detail (focal point)** - the single highest-detail/contrast area that owns the eye.
- **E. Color & power-FX signature** - identity color + accents; value structure; how the POWER
  reads in color + shape.
- **F. Asymmetry & the one-of-a-kind** - what's mismatched / custom / unique (asymmetry = memorable).
- **G. Function & culture** - why each piece is there (use); what motif / era / origin shows in
  pattern & ornament.
- **H. Motion & flow** - what trails / moves (hair, cloth, tail, FX); the "epic" element in motion.
- **I. Juxtaposition** - which opposites are paired (delicate+brutal, clean+filthy, organic+mechanical).
- **J. Read at 3 distances** - far (silhouette) / mid (color+shapes) / close (texture): works at all three?

## How to run it
1. After identity is locked, pick the ~4-8 buckets that are real choices for THIS character.
2. Ask them as structured questions (2-4 curated, evocative, character-specific options each;
   AskUserQuestion allows 4 questions/call, so batch in rounds).
3. Lock answers into the character bible's Layers 4-5 + an ART-DEPTH summary.
4. Iterate on the generated image - deepening a boundary/seam, unifying a costume, adding intricacy
   (as we did with ZERO: ice<->flesh interweave + unified cosmic garment) is part of the pass.

## Lesson (from ZERO)
The richest details came from pushing on **boundaries/seams** ("what happens WHERE ice meets
flesh?") and on **unifying + fictionalizing the costume** ("uniform, creative, cosmic-alien" beat
"tattered rag"). Ask about the transitions and the coherence, not just the parts.
