## 0. THE PITCH / PLAYER FANTASY
**Be the unbreakable wall.** You don't chase and you don't dodge - you plant, you endure, and
everything that hits you breaks first. The immovable protector: the enemy cannot get past you,
and the longer the fight goes, the more it's yours. For the player who wants to outlast, absorb,
and win by refusing to fall.

## 1. THE SEED + THE CONTRADICTION (the heart)
A colossal **guardian statue** that woke with a conscience. **The most destructive body in the
arena, driven by a heart that refuses to destroy.** He was raised to PROTECT - and he was made
to level the very thing he guarded. Everything downstream hangs on this single tension:
destruction incarnate, wielded by someone who now only wants to shield.

## 2. THE WOUND & THE WANT
- **Wound:** he destroyed what he was built to protect. (Turned/used as a weapon against his own
  charge - the exact mechanism is open, but the guilt is the engine.) He carries every stone of
  it.
- **Want (his OVERTHRONE wish - LOCKED):** **to restore what he destroyed.** The unclaimed wish
  is the only thing in the world big enough to undo what he did. That is why a being who hates to
  fight enters a fighting league at all - it is the one door back.
- **Fear:** being made to destroy again. Losing control a second time.

## 3. THE WAY THEY FIGHT (bridge to mechanics - LOCKED)
He fights **purely defensively, on purpose.** He absorbs, he endures, he **shoves you back
("you shall not pass") but never strikes to kill.** A fight, to ATLAS, is not a contest - it is
**penance**: hold the line, take the blows, prove a weapon can choose to only guard. His skill /
stance is a wall, not a weapon.
- **Origin of skill:** not trained, not instinct - **built.** He is a made thing whose every
  design was for force; his discipline is the refusal to use it as intended.
- Maps cleanly to the TANK kit: high HP + slow = his mass and reluctance; defensive stance =
  his vow; knockback-shove = "get back, I will not hurt you, but you shall not pass." The kit
  IS the character.

## 9. VOICE
Slow, deep, few words, weary and gentle. His "taunts" are warnings or apologies, never threats.
- Warning: *"Turn back."* / *"I don't want to do this."*
- Win (quiet, sad): *"...Still standing. I'm sorry."*
- Lose: *"...Good. Let me rest."*
- Catchphrase / core line: *"You shall not pass - and neither will I strike."*
