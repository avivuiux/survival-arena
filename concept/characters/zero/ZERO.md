## Layer 0 - THE PITCH / PLAYER FANTASY (DRAFT)
The untouchable **control master**: freeze the chaos, dictate every exchange, never be rattled.
You pick ZERO to out-think and out-last, not out-rush - ice-cold dominance. (Opposite of FANG's
reckless-aggression fantasy.)

## Layer 1 - THE SEED (LOCKED)
**He went cold to survive.** A man who gained ice powers and was trapped in ice for years; he woke
in the Circuit era to find everyone and everything he knew gone. He is the composed, unshakable
veteran precisely *because he already lost everything once* - nothing here can hurt him like that
did. The twist: the ice that took his world is the same ice he now commands - he is **master AND
prisoner of the same power.** <- Aviv (got powers + frozen for years).
**Refinement (Aviv 2026-07-01) - the merge + a darker turn:** the ice combined with something
**cosmic / supernatural**, and that fusion made him **MERGE with the ice** and become the cosmic
ice entity he is now. The merge did not leave him neutral - it made him **slightly malevolent and
cold-tempered.** A sliver of the man was replaced by something colder and a little cruel; he edges
toward antagonist (helpfully filling the roster's missing "villain" lean, sharpening warm-FANG vs
cold-ZERO).
**Contrast to FANG:** FANG = a warm heart the world fears (monster outside, soft inside); ZERO =
a cold surface hiding loss (calm outside, frozen grief inside).

## Layer 2 - THE WOUND & THE WANT (LOCKED)
- **Wound:** frozen out of his own time - woke to a world where everything and everyone he knew is
  gone. He survived by going cold, inside and out. <- Layer 1.
- **Want (LOCKED, Aviv):** **to restore what the ice took** - turn back the loss, his time/world/
  people. His wish to the champion = undo it.
- **Fear:** that it is truly unrecoverable - that even if he wins, he stays frozen inside forever.
- **Plot hook:** the want links straight to the legend/boss (the legend may be tied to his
  freezing, or to the lost world he's chasing). <- CONCEPT: ZERO's wish ties to what he lost.

## Layer 3 - THE WAY HE FIGHTS (LOCKED archetype, derived detail)
**Control / zone.** Freezes the stage, slows the enemy, dictates tempo - patient, calculating,
defensive. The exact opposite of FANG's reckless pounce. <- balanced/chill archetype (greybox).
- Origin: years spent mastering the ice that once mastered him; precision born of a man with
  nothing left to lose. <- Layer 1.
- What a fight means to him: control is the one thing he still has - so he takes it, totally.

## Layer 4 - THE BODY (LOCKED - via ART-DEPTH pass; INVERSION 2026-07-01)
- Tall, lean, ATHLETIC control-fighter build, NOT bulky. <- control not brawn; contrast to FANG.
- **THE INVERSION (Aviv):** he is **almost ENTIRELY living crystalline ICE** now - a cosmic ice
  being - and the **human flesh is the small REMNANT** (exactly one HALF of his face + ONE arm).
  The ice didn't just touch him; it nearly WON. This deepens the seed: now almost-all-prisoner,
  a fragment of the man still inside. (Earlier draft had it reversed; the inversion is canon.)
- **Cosmic body:** faint galaxy/nebula and star-flecks glimmer INSIDE the translucent ice;
  fine constellation etchings across the crystal. <- cosmic-alien direction (Aviv).
- **Intricate ice<->flesh boundary:** at the seams the ice INTERWEAVES with the living flesh -
  fractal cracks and branching ice-veins creep across the human skin, small shards emerge through
  it, frost-lace laces the transition. NOT a clean line. <- Aviv (the key intricacy).
- **He FLOATS** - hovers elegantly off the ground. <- roster-float direction (Aviv).
- An **ice crown**; cold pale-blue glowing eye(s); calm, unreadable, still face. <- Layer 1.
- Crystalline surface: smooth-yet-spiky, glittering, angular. <- shape-language.
